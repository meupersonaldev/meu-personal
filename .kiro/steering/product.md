# Meu Personal - Product Overview

## What It Is
A SaaS platform for managing personal training services in franchised gyms (academies) in Brazil. The system connects students, personal trainers (professors), franchises (franquias), and the franchisor (franqueadora).

## User Roles
- **Aluno (Student)**: Books sessions with personal trainers, purchases credit packages, performs check-ins at academies
- **Professor (Teacher)**: Manages availability, accepts bookings, tracks students, handles wallet/earnings
- **Franquia (Franchise)**: Individual gym location that manages teachers, students, schedules, and finances
- **Franqueadora (Franchisor)**: Parent company that oversees all franchises, manages policies, approves teachers, and handles leads
